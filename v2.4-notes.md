# Millie 2.4 — security & privacy update

**Ask Milly is now context-aware about your privacy.** Page content is never shared with cloud AI providers (Claude, GPT, Gemini) from private Spaces, internal Millie pages, or local files. A new **Settings → AI → "Share current page with AI"** toggle lets you turn page sharing off entirely — your question is always sent, the page's text only when you allow it.

**Sync sign-in is hardened.** Your sync session token now lives in the macOS Keychain instead of app preferences, protecting your account if your Mac is compromised. Existing sign-ins carry over automatically.
