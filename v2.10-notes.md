# Millie 2.10

**Premium streaming now works out of the box.** DirecTV Stream, Netflix, and other services that use protected (DRM) video now play in Millie — no setup, no flags. Millie bundles the Widevine module and installs it automatically on first launch.

**Tabs keep their icons.** Restored and sleeping tabs no longer fall back to a blank globe — favicons are cached and rehydrated, and sleeping tabs show their icon dimmed instead of losing it.

**Under the hood.** Certificate-revocation data (CRLSet) refreshes again, and the Chrome Web Store "Add to Millie" installer is aligned with the current engine version.
